About game 2048

The purpose of the game is to match the numbers together (number power of 2) in order to reach the End tile '2048' and win the game!

2048 game area 4x4 grid of 16 square slots. At the start of the game, you have two squares (also called "tiles") with the number '2' inside.

When you make 2 tiles with the same number inside join, they merge into a new one from the number that does not add 2 previous : 2 +2 = 4, 4 +4 = 8, ... 1024 +1024 = 2048 !

To move the tiles on the grid, you just have to choose a direction (top, right, bottom, or left). All tiles move in the selected direction until they merge with a tile that has the same number or are blocked by tiles with a different number.

On a computer, just use the arrow keys on the keyboard 4 . On a mobile device with a touch interface (for example, an iOS or Android device), swipe in the desired direction.
